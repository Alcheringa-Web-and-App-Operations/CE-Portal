About
Alcheringa
Alcheringa, popularly known as “Alcher” is the annual socio-cultural festival of Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Guwahati.Started in 1996 by a group of students of IIT Guwahati, Alcheringa has now become one of the biggest college level events in Asia. The name derives from the Australian language, literally translating to ‘Eternal Dreamtime’ and hence befitting the moment in every sense of the word.
